# Privacy Policy for Last Lie

**Last updated:** May 22, 2026

## Overview

Last Lie is a personal honesty tracker. It is designed with one principle: your data is yours, and it never leaves your device.

This privacy policy explains what information the app handles, how it is stored, and what we do — and do not — do with it.

## The short version

- Last Lie does not collect any personal data.
- Last Lie does not require an account.
- Last Lie does not connect to the internet.
- Last Lie does not contain ads.
- Last Lie does not contain trackers or analytics.
- Everything you write stays on your device.
- When you delete the app, all your data is permanently erased.

## Information we do not collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any of the following:

- Your name, email address, phone number, or any contact information
- Your location
- Your IP address
- Your device identifier or advertising ID
- The content of your entries (categories, notes, dates)
- Crash reports or diagnostic data
- Usage statistics
- Cookies or any form of tracking

## Information stored on your device

Last Lie stores the following data locally on your device using the operating system's standard local storage (SharedPreferences on Android, UserDefaults on iOS):

- The list of entries you create, including the date, category, and optional note
- Your selected language preference

This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to any of our servers, because we do not operate any servers. It is not synced to any cloud, because the app has no sync functionality.

If your device's operating system performs its own automatic backup (such as iCloud Backup on iOS or Google One backup on Android), Last Lie's data may be included in that backup. This is controlled entirely by your device and your account settings with Apple or Google, not by Last Lie. We have no access to those backups.

## Data deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

1. Deleting individual entries from within the app, or
2. Uninstalling the app from your device.

When you uninstall Last Lie, all data created by the app is permanently removed from your device by the operating system.

## Permissions

Last Lie does not request any system permissions. It does not access:

- Your camera
- Your microphone
- Your contacts
- Your photos
- Your location
- Your storage outside the app's private sandbox

## Children's privacy

Last Lie does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. Because the app collects no data and requires no account, it is compliant with COPPA and similar regulations by design. The app is rated for users aged 12 and over due to the reflective nature of its content.

## Third-party services

Last Lie does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools.

## Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at the same URL where you found this document. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the date of any changes.

Because Last Lie does not collect data, future versions of this policy will only become more permissive, not less.
